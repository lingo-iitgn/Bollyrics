

 ye meri zindagi bejaan laash thi
barason se pyaar ko teri talaash thi
bas aaj meri khoyi taqadir mil gayi
teri tasavir mil gayi

ab ke bahaar men is intazaar men
dil kaa vo haal thaa mushakil ye saal thaa
divaane ke liye ik zanjir mil gayi
teri tasavir mil gayi

tasavir men magar ye kaun hai bataa
is ko zaraa uthaa mujhako yahaan bithaa
kyaa kaam is kaa raanjhe ko hir mil gayi
teri tasavir mil gayi

aayi hai tuut kar is par javaaniyaan
bachapan ki hai magar saari nishaaniyaan
mere hasin kvaabon ki taabir mil gayi
teri tasavir mil gayi
