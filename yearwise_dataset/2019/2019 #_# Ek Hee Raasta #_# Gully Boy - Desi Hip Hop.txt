

 ek hi rasta jisape chup chaap sar ko jhukaaye hue
band ankhen kiye log chalate he saare janam
jaanate bhi nahi sochate bhi nahi poochhate bhi nahi
unako ye rastaa lekar kahin jaayegaa
yaa kahin bhi nahi

chalate chalate kahin ek mode aata he
sidhe raste se bilkul alag
koi divaana hi hota he jo kii udhar jaataa he
varanaa baaki to sab sidhe raste pe hi
apane saare janam chalate hai

sar jhukaaye hue band ankhen kiye or ye dukh liye
mode jo dekha tha usape mud jate ham
to najaane kahan tak pahunch pate ham
