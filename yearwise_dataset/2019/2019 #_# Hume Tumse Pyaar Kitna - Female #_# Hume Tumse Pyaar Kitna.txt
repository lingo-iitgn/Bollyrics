

 tumhen dekhake hi ye saansen chalengi
tumhaare bina ab naa ye ankhen khulengi
meri bekaraari kyun tum nahin maanate

hamen tumse pyaar kitna  ye ham nahin jaanate
magar ji nahin sakate tumhaare bina
hamen tumse pyaar kitna

tumhen koi or dekhe  to jalataa hai dil
badi mushkilon se phir samhalta hai dil
kyakya jatan karte hai  tumhen kya pataa

ye dil bekaraar kitna  ye ham nahin jaanate
magar ji nahin sakate tumhaare bina
hamen tumse pyaar kitna

suna gam judaai kaa uthaate hai log
jane zindagi kaise bitaate hai log
din bhi yahan to lage baras ke samaan

hamen intazaar kitna  ye ham nahin jaanate
magar ji nahin sakate tumhaare bina
hamen tumse pyaar kitna
