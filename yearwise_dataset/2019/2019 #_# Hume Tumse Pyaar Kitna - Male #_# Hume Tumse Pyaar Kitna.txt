

 tumhen dekh ke hi ye saansen chaylgi
tumhaare bina ab naa ye ankhen khuilgi
meri bekaraari kyun tum nahin maanate

hamen tumse pyaar kitna  ye ham nahin jaanate
magar ji nahin sakate tumhaare bina
hamen tumse pyaar

tumhen koi or dekhe  to jayeelata hai diqul
badi mushkiqul se phir samhakalata hai diqul
kya kya jatan karte hai  tumhen kya pataa
ye diqul bekaraar kitna  ye ham nahin jaanate
magar ji nahin sakate tumhaare bina
hamen tumse pyaar

suna gam judaai kaa uthaate hai laga
jane zindagi kaise bitaate hai laga
din bhi yahan to lage baras ke samaa
hamen intazaar kitna  ye ham nahin jaanate
magar ji nahin sakate tumhaare bina
hamen tumse pyaar
