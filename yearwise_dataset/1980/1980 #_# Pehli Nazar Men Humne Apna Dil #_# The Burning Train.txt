

   
  
pehalee najar me hamne toh apana dil de diya tha tumko
par tumne der lagaayee ruk ruk ke baat badhaayee
waise to humne milte hi tumse apnaa liyaa tha tumko
par jaan ke baat chupaaye naa kahe ke kadar bhadhaai

dekha tumhe toh hamne sabse najar utha lee
khone kee chij kho lee paane kee chij pa lee
ho o sidhe naa sahee ghum ke sahee mil toh gaye hain
pehalee najar me hamne toh apana dil de diya tha tumko
par tumne der lagaayee ruk ruk ke baat badhaayee

hamne tumhaaree khaatir kya kya kiye bahaane
ho o aasaan nahee mohabbat abb toh yeh baat maanee
ho o chhoda nahee dam jaise bhee ho ham mil toh gaye hain
pehalee najar me hamne toh apana dil de diya tha tumko
par tumne der lagaayee ruk ruk ke baat badhaayee

likha tha aasmaan par yu hee yeh khel hona
pehle najar ulajhaana phir dil kaa mel hona
ho o chhodo yeh gile jaise bhee mile mil toh gaye hain
pehalee najar me hamne toh apana dil de diya tha tumko
par tumne der lagaayee ruk ruk ke baat badhaayee
