

 parakhna matparakhne mein koyii aapna nahiin rehta
kissi bhii aaeene mein der tak chehra nahiin rehta

bade logon se milne mein hamesha faasrakhna
jahaa dariya samandar se miladariya nahiin rehta

toumhaara shahar to bilkul naye andaaz walaa hain
humare shahar mein bhi ab koyii hum sa nahiin rehta

mohabbat mein ek khushboun hainhamesha saath chalti hain
koyii insaaan tanhaai mein bhii tanhaaa nahiin rehta
