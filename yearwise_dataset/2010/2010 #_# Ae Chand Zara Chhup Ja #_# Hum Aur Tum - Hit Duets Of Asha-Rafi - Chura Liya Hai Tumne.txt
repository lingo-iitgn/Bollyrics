

 aye chaand zara chhup ja ai vaqt zara ruk ja
ek baat hai honthon pe kah loon to qaraar aaye
ek bojh to hat jaaye
aye chaand zara chhup ja ai vaqt zara ruk ja
ruk unaka idhar ko hai ab kyon na bahaar aaye
duniya na badal jaaye
aye chaand zara chhup 
sab kvaab sa lagata hai is raat mein jaadu hai
kaamosh nazaaron ki har saans mein jaadu hai
ham hain ki bandhe se hain dil hai ki uda jaaye
ek baat hai honthon 
main pyaar ki baazi mein dil haara to kya haara 
pahalu mein mere duniya baanhon mein jahaan saara
kya dega muqaddar ab ham maang ke pachhataaye
aye chaand zara
