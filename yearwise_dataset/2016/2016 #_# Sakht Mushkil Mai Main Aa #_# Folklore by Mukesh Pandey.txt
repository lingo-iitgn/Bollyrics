

 mithe ras se bhari re radha rani laage
mane kaaro kaaro jamunaji ro pani laage 
yamuna maiyaa kaari kaari radha gori gori 
vrindavan main dhooma machaave barsaanaa ri chhori 
vrajdham radhaju kii rajadhaani laage 
kanha nit murali me terre sumare baram baar 
kotine rup dhare manmohan tau naa paave paar 
rup rang kii chhabili pataraani laage 
naa bhaave mane maakhanmisari ab naa koi mithai 
maari jibadya ne bhaave ab to radha naam malai 
vrishabhanu kii laali to guddhani laage 
radha radha naam ratt he jo nar aathon yam 
tinaki baadha dur karat he radha radha naam 
radha naam me safal jindagaani laage
