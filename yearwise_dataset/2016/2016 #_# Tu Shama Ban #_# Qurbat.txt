

 tu shamaa ban main hawa ho jaaun
tere har gam kii dawa ho jaaun

itani qurbat ho dhadkane gin la
yun lipat jaaun qabaa ho jaaun

phir na aae khyashel phurqat kaa
main tere ghar kaa pataa ho jaaun

justaju ban ke tu rahe diqul main
main tere diqul kii dua ho jaaun

tu kabhi meri jubaan ban  koshish
main kabhi teri sadaa ho jaaun
