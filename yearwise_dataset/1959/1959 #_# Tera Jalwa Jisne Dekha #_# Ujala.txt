

 teraa jalawaa jisane dekhaa wo teraa ho gayaa
mai ho gayee kisee kee koee meraa ho gayaa
kyaa dekhaa thaa tuz mein dil teraa ho gayaa
ye sochate hee sochate saweraa ho gayaa

hai muz mein bhee ayesaa anokhaa saa jaadoo
jo dekhe wo hotaa hai dil se bekaaboo
lage tose nainaa to nainaa naa laage
khayaalon mein doobee hoo tere o baaboo
too aayaa to mahafil mein ujaalaa ho gayaa

teree yek zalak ne wo haalat banaa dee
mere tanabadan mein mohabbat jagaa dee
kabhee bhoolakar to idhar bah de paanee
kinaare pen rahatee hoo fir bhee main pyaasee
ab dil kaa kyo kare gam gayaa so gayaa

bulaatee hain tuz ko ye aanchal kee chhaiyyaa
jaraa muskuraa de padoo tore paiyyaa
kasam hain tuze dil kee jaanayetamannaa
tuze hum ne maanaa hai apanaa hee saiyyaa
mai akelee aaur teraa jamaanaa ho gayaa
