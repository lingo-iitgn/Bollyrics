

 o bekarar dil ho chuka hain mujhko aansu se pyar
mujhe too khushee na de nayee jindagee na de

milee chaman ko bahar hasee phul ko milee
git koyal ko mile aur maine payee khamoshee
mujhe basuree na de koyee raginee na de
o bekarar dil

aaye ghata ghir ke ghata chhaye
aur pyasee kalee gam kee jalee taras taras jaye
rahe sada jo meraa wahee meree jindagee
hain roj andhera aur char din kee chandanee
mujhe chandanee na de mujhe roshanee na de
o bekarar dil
