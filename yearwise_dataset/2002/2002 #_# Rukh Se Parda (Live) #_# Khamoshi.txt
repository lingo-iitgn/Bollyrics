

 rukh se parda utha de zara sakiya
bas abhi range mehfil badal jayega
hai jo behosh vo hosh mein aayega
girne wahai jo sambhal jayge

tum tasalli na do sirf baithe raho
wakt kuch mere marne ka tal jayega
kya yeh kam hai masiha ke rahne hi se
maut ka bhi irada badal jayega

teer ki jaan hai dil dil ki jaan teer hai
teer ko na yun khicho kaha maan lo
teer khicha to dil bhi nikal aayega
dil jo nikto dum bhi nikal jayega

iske hasne main rone ka aandaz hai
khak udane main fariyad ka raaz hai
isko chedo na aanvar khuda ke liye
warna bimar ka dum nikal jayega
