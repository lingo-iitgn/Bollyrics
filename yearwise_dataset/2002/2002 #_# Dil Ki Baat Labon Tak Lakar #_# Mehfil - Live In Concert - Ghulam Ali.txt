

 dil kii baat labon parkar ab tak ham dukh sehate hai
hamane suna tha is basti main dilavaale bhi rahete hai

ek hamen aawaara kahana koi bada ilzaam nahin
duniya vaale dilawaalon ko or bahut kuch kehete hai

biit gaya sawan kaa maheenaa mausam ne nazaren badalin
lekin in pyaasi aankhon se ab tak ansu behate hai

jinaki khaatir shahar bhi choda jinke liye badnaam huye
aaj wohi hamase begaane begaane se rahete hai

wo jo abhi is rahaguzar se chaakgirebaan guzara tha
us aawaara diwane ko jaalib jaalib kehete hai
