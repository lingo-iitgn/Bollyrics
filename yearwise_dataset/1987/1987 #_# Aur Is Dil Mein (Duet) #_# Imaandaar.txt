

 aur is dil mein kya rakha hai
tera hi dard chhupa rakha hai
cheer ke dekhe dil mera to
tera hi naam likha rakha hai

pyar ke afasane sune the logon se
pyar kya hota hai ye tune samajhaya
midil tujh se to khwab dekhe aise
junoon jaane kaisa jawaan dil pe chaaya
dil mein aisa dard utha dil ho gaya diwana
diwanon ne is duniyan mein dard kanam dawan rakha hai

nigahon mein meri ye surat hain teri
zindagi ye meri amanat hain teri
dhadakte seene mein mohabbat hain teri
mohabbat ye teri ibadat hain meri
tere siva kuch yaad nahi hai tu hi tu dil me

dil ne yaar ki pooja ki hai pyar ka naam khuda rakha hai
