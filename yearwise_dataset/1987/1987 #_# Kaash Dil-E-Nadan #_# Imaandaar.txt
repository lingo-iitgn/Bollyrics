vahi aag seene main phir jal padi he

kuch aise hi din the wo jab ham mili the
chaman main nahin phool dil main khile the
vahi to he mausam magar rut nahin wo
mere saath barsaat bhi ro padi he

koi kaash dil pe zaraa haath rakh de
mere dil ke tukdon ko ek saath rakh de
magar ye he khwaabon khayalon kii baaten
kabhi tut kar chiz koi judy he
