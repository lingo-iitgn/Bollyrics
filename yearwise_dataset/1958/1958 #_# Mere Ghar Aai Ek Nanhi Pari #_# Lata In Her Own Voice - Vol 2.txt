chandani ke hasin rath pe sawar

usake aane se mere aangan me khil uthhe phul gungunayee bahar
dekh kar usako jee nahee bharata chahe dekhu use hajar bar

maine puchha use ke kaun hain too has ke bolee ke mai hoo teraa pyar
mai tere dil me thee hamesha se ghar me aayee hu aaj pahalee bar
