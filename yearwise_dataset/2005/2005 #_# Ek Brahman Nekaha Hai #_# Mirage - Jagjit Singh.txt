

 ek brahman ne kaha hai ki ye saal accha hai
zulm ki raat bahut jaldi dhalegi ab aag chulhe me har ek roz jalegi ab to
bhookh ke maare koi baccha nahi chain ki neend har ek shaks yahan soyega
aandhi nafrat ki chalegi na kahin ab ke pyaar ki fasl ugaayegi zameen ab ke baras
hai yakeen ab na koi shor sharaba zulm hoga na kahin khoon kharaba hoga
os aur dhoop ke sadme na sahega ab mere des me beghar na rahega koi
naye vaadon ka jo daahai wo jaal accha rehnumaaon ne kaha hai ki ye saal accha humko maalum hai jannat ki haqeeqat lekin
dil ke khush rakhne ko ghalib ye khayal accha hai
