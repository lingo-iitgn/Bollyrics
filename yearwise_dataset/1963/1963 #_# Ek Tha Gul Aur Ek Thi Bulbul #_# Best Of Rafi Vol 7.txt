

 ek tha gul or ek thi bulbul
donon chaman main rahete the he ye kahani bilkul sachhee
mere naanaa kehete the bulbul kuch aise gaati thi jaise tum baaten karti ho
wo gul aise sharmaata tha
jaise main ghabara jaataa ha
bulbul ko maaloom nahin tha gul aise kyo sharmaata tha
wo kya jane uskaa naguya gul ke dil ko dhadakata tha
dil ke bhed naa aate lab pe ye dil main hi rahete the
lekin aakhir dil kii baaten aise kitne din chhupati hai
ye wo kaliyaan hai jo ek din bas kaante ban ke chubhti hai

ek din jaan liya bulbul ne wo gul us kaa divaana he
tum ko pasand aayaa ho to bolu phir aage jo afasana he
ek duje kaa ho jane par wo dono majaboor huye
un dono ke pyaar ke kisse gulshan main mashhoor huye
saath jiyenge saath marenge wo dono ye kehete the
phir ek din kii baat sunaaun ek sayyad chaman main aayaa
le gaya wo bulbul ko pakad ke
or divaana gul murajhaayaa
shaayar log bayaan karte hai
aise un kii judaai kii baaten
gaate te ye geet wo dono
saiya bina nahin katati raaten
mast bahaaron kaa mausam tha aankh se aansu behate the
aati thi awaz hameshaa
ye jhilmil jhilmil taaron se
jiskaa naam mohabbat he wo
kab ruukti he diwaron se
ek din aah gulobulbul kii
us pinjade se ja takaraai
tutaa pinjadaa chuta kaidi detaa rahaa saiyyaad duhai
roak sake naa us ko milke
sara jamaana sari khudaai
gul saajan ko geet sunaane
bulbul baag main waapas ai
yaad sadaa rakhana ye kahani
chaahe jeena chaahe maranaa
tum bhi kisi se pyaar karo to
pyaar gulobulbul sa karnaa
