

 yeh zameen ga rahi hain asmaan ga raha hai
saath mere ye saara jahaan ga raha hai

shokh kaliyon ke ghoonghat sarakane lage hain
mast phoolon ke dil bhi dhadakane lage hai
ye bahaaron ka dilkash samaa ga raha hai

bhool kar raah koi haseen aa na jaaye
is jagah koi parada nashin aa na jaaye
is jagah aaj ek naujawan ga raha haisaath mere ye saara jahaan gaa raha hai
