

 aap ke anurodh par main ye geet sunaata hun
apane dil ki baaton se aap ka dil bahalaata hun
aap ke anurodh pe
mat poochho auron ke dukh men ye prem kavi kyon rota hai
bas chot kisi ko lagati hai aur dard kisi ko hota hai
dur kahin koi darpan toote tadap ke main rah jaata hun
aap ke anurodh pe
taareef main jis ki karata hun kya roop hai vo kya khushboo hai
kuchh baat nahin aisi koi ye ek suron ka jaadu hai
koyal ki ek kook se sab ke dil men uthaata hun
aap ke anurodh pe
main pahene phirata hun jo vo zanjeeren kaise banati hain
ye bhed bata dun geeton men tasaveeren kaise banati hain
sundar honthon ki laali se main rang roop churaata hun
aap ke anurodh pe main ye geet sunaata hun
