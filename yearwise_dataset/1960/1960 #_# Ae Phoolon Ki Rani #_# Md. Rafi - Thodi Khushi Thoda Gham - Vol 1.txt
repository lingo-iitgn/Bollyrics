

 i phulon kii rani bahaaron kii malikaa
tera muskuraana gazab ho gaya
na dil hosh main he na ham hosh main hai
nazar kaa milaanaa gazab ho gaya

tere hont kya hai gulabi kanwal hai
ye do pattiyaan pyaar kii ek gazal he
wo najuk labon se muhabbat kii baaten
hameen ko sunaanaa gazab ho gaya
i phulon kii rani
kabhi khul ke milanaa kabhi khud jhijakna
kabhi raaston par bahakanamachalanaa
ye palkon kii chilman uthaakar giraanaa
giraakar uthaanaa gazab ho gaya
i phulon kii rani
fizaaon main thandak gata par javaani
tere gesuon kii badi meharbaani
har ek pench main saikadon maikade hai
tera ladkhadana gazab ho gaya
i phulon kii rani
