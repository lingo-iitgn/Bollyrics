

 ye raat jaise dulhan ban gayi hai chiraagon se
karunga ujaalaa main dil ke daagon se

aaj kee raat mere  dil kee salaami le le
dil kee salaami le le
kal teri bazm se deewaanaa chalaa jayega
shamma rahe jaaegi paravaanaa chalaa jayega
  
teri mehafil tere jalwe hon mubarak tujko
teri ulfat se nahin aaj bhee inkaar muje
tera maykhaanaa salaamat rahe i jaanevafaa
muskuraakar too zaraa dekh le ik baar muje
phir tere pyaar kaa mastaana chalaa jayega
  
maine chaha ki bataa don main haqiqat apni
toone lekin na mera raajemuhabbat samajha
meri uljhan mere halaat yahaan tak pahunche
teri aankhon ne mere pyaar ko nafrat samajha
ab teri raah se begaana chalaa jayega
  
too mera saath na de raahemuhabbat mein sanam
chalatechalate main kisi raah pe mud jaoonga
kehkashan chand sitare tere chuumenge qadam
tere raste kee main ek dhul hoon  ud jaoonga
saath mere mera afasaana chalaa jayega
