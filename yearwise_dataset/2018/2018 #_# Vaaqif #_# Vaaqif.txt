

 usulon kii jo thi duniya ab hai kahan bolo
baaten kitaabi jo suni hamen na yakeen dekho

guftgu aise mazahabi par ab mujhako gumaan
hoon waakif na hoon nadaan koi na fikarey jahaan

kyun naamanzoor o hujur kirdaar hai yahan
is kahani kaa na magarur bekasoor hoon gunjati hai jo dil kii jubaan

kahata  koi thi raushnee jahaan
ab hai baaki jalataa ashiyaan
naasamajh tujko mubarak ye girtaa jahaan

hai munasib har anjaam waakif hoon na nadaan

ye manzar hai agar par yaad hai kahan
poochhun main yahan o mahfuj belgaum armaanon main dubi begraj udaan
