

 na sawal banke mikaro na jawab banke mikaro
meri zindagi mere khwaab hain mujhe khwab banke mikaro

har ek shaam ko mere dosto na azaab banke mikaro
mujhe mehqadeh mein milo agar to sharab banke mikaro

mujhe achhe log bahut mile main to unke qarz se mar gaya
agar ho sake to kabhi kabhi to kharab banke mikaro

abhi sochna hai to soch lo hamein abhi chhodna hai to chhod do
naye mausamon mein milo mujhe to gulab banke mikaro

na to is tarah se mikaro na to us tarahse mikaro
haan hazoor banke mikaro haan janaab banke mikaro
