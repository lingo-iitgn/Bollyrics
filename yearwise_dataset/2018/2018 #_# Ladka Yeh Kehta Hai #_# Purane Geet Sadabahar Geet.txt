

 husn ka mara hu dil se begana hu mastana ajnabee thoda sa divana hu
mehakee hawao ne mujhko bulaya hain pariyo kee nagaree me pardesee aaya hain
ladka yeh kehta hai ladki se hoton me dabee dabee hasi se
aaya hu dur se karne mai dostee hath milo ajnabee se
jan lo man lo chehra meraa pehchan lo ladka yeh kehta hai ladki se

aap ka sath toh suhana hain baton me meetha tara hain
dil ke kono me bitha lo jee kitna akedivana hai
ladka yeh kehta hai ladki se de do jawab mujhe jaldi se
aaya hu dur se karne mai dostee hath milo ajnabee se
jan lo man lo chehra meraa pehchan lo ladka yeh kehta hai ladki se
