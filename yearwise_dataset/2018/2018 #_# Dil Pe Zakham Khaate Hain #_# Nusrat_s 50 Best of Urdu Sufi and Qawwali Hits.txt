

 dil pe zakhm khaate hain jaan se guzarte hain
jurm sirf itna hai unko pyaar karte hain
aitbaar badhta hai aur bhi mohabbat ka
jab woh ajnabi bankar paas se guzarte hain
unki anjuman bhi hai tar bhi rasan bhi hai
dekhna hai deewaane ab kahaan theherte hain
unke ik tagaful se tootte hain dil kitne
unki ik tawwajjo se kitne zakhm bharte hain
jo pale hain zulmat mein kya sehar ko pechhanein
teergi ke shaydaayi raushni se darte hain
laakh wo gurezaan hon laakh dushmanejaan hon
dil ko kya karein saahib hum unhi pe marte hain
