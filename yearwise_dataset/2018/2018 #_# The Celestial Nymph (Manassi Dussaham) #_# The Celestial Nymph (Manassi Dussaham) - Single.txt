

 panimati mukhi bale padmanabhan innennil
kanivillaykayal kaman parameyyunnu

ekanta yamam snehardha ragam novai padarneelayo
param vimookam kannil vibhatam maralayakunnuvo
nin shyama varna chorunnee vanam theyay padarneelayo
ennatma thapam meera vilapam kelkkathilinjeelayo
ee rasa kelee ravil marandam nathan thodaykil neerunnovo
natha ninne thedi pranan pidanjeeduvathen arike

loka vasikalkellam lobhaniyanamindu
shokam enikku matram sumukhi tarunnadende
ekanthattilennodo sakam cheyda leelagal
akave mama kantham ashu vaka maranno
manasi dusshaham ayyo madana gadanam
endu madirakshi jnyan cheyyavu
