

 dono hi mohabbat ke jazbaat me jalate hai
vo barf pe chalate hai ham aag pe chalate hai
ehasaas ki shikast se kuchh ashq nikalate hai

zindagi hai aur dilenaadaan hai
kyaa safar hai aur kyaa saamaan hai
mere gamo ko bhi samajh kar dekhie
muskuraa denaa bahut aasaan hai
ehasaas ki shikast se

ye barf ke tukade hai garmi se pighalate hai
ek raat thahar jaae ham ghar me tere lekin
tumhaare ghar daravaazaa hai lekin tumhe khatare kaa adaazaa nahi hai
hame khatare kaa adaazaa hai lekin hamaare ghar daravaazaa me nahi hai
ek raat thahar jaae

mere kaam kaa hai na duniyaa ke kaam kaa
are dil hi tumhe khudaa ne diyaa das graam kaa
ek raat thahar jaae

chhat par na sulaa denaa ham nid me chalate hai
chori ki mohabbat me aksar yahi hotaa hai
aadami kaa hai aajakal haal ek sahami si chaah ho jaise
aur log yu chhup ke pyaar karate hai ki pyaar karanaa gunaah ho jaise
chori ki mohabbat me

yu to hansate hue ladako ko bhi gam hotaa hai
kachchi umro me magar tajurbaa kam hotaa hai
chori ki mohabbat me

daravaaze se jaate hai khidaki se nikalate hai
jis din se hui shaadi ye haal hamaaraa hai
haldi se darate hai mehadi se ghabaraate hai
ummidevafaa rakhe kyaa unase bhalaa koi
na jikr kijie meri adaa ke baare me
aur sunaa hai vo bhi muhabbat kaa shauq rakhane lage
jinhe khabar hi nahi vafaa ke baare me
ummidevafaa rakhe
