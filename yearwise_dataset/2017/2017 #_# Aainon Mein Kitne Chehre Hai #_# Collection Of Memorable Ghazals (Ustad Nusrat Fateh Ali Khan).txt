

 aaeeno mein yeh kitne chehre hain
aqs tere hain aqs mere hain

itne pathar agar baham ho jaye
rafte ko aasmaan lagein

itne zarre hon agar jama kahin
wo sate dasht e benishaan lagein

itne taarein agar chamakte hon
zarf e kurshid bechirag lage

itne unche agar mehakte hon
mausam e zard besurag lage

neel se taab aakash agar dekho
ek hi roshni ke ghere hain

sab musafir hain ek hi raste ke
ek manzil pe sabko jaaan hai

apni apni hadon mein rehte hue
ek naya dayara banana hai

roshni jo hira se tooti thi
usmein rehna hai jagmagaana hai

najd se uthne wali khushboo ka
phir se ab muntazir zamaana hai

taara taara bikharte jaayenge
jitne saaye hain jo andhere hain
