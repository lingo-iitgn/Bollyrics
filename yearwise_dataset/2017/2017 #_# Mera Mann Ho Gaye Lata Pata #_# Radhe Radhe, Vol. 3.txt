

 tede sundar nain tede mukh kahe bain
tedho hi mukut baat tedi kachu kah gayo
tede ghunghraare bal tedi gal phoolmaal
tede hi bulaak mere chit main based gayo

tede pag upar nipur jhankaar karen

tedi bansuri bajaay chit chure gayo
aise tede tedan ko dhyaan dhare mayaram



banke bihari kii dekh chata



are banke bihari kii dekh chata


kab se khoju banavaari ko


koi bataa de uskaa pataa


mor mukut shyaamal tan dhari


or qamar main baandhe pilo pata


paniya bharan yamuna tat aayin





tedi nazre lat ghungharaari





milte he use banke bihari
