

 tumhare khat mein naya ek salaam kiska tha
na tha raqueeb toh aakhir woh naam kiska tha

raha na dil mein woh bedard aur dard raha
mukim kon hua hai makaam kiska tha

wafa karenge nibhayenge baat maanege
tumhe bhi yaad kuch ye kalaam kiska tha

guzar gaya woh zamana kahoo toh kisse kahoo
khyaal dil ko mere subahoshaam kiska tha

har ek se kehate hai kya daag bewafa nikala
ye puchhe inse koi woh ghulam kiska tha
