

 tenu itnaa main pyaar karaan
ek pal wich sau baar karaan
tu jaave je menu chhadd ke
maut da intazaar karaan
ke tere liye duniyaa chhod di hai
tujpe hee saans aake ruke
main tujko kithna chahta hum
ye tu kabhi soch naa sake

kuch bhee nahin hai ye jahaan
too hai to hai isamen zindagi
ab mujko janaa hai kahaan
ke tu hi safar he aakiri
ke tere bina jeenaa mumkin nahin
na dena kabhi mujhako tu faasale
main tujko kitna chaahati hoon
ye tu kabhi soch naa sake

ke chehre se tere naa hate
