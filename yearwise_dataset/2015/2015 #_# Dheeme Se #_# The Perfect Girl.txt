poetry
ye dil hi hai ya dil mein koi aur hai
ye jo bheegi boondhein in palkon se bhichdi hain
teri beeti hui yaadon ki sooni galiyon se guzri hain
ye muskurahat jo honthon mein chhupaye ho tum
ye gham ki konsi aafat uthaye ho tum
khayalon ke is narm mulayam chadar par
ye kisne basera banaya hai
shayad tum hi ho

chal rahi hai koshishein
rooh ko raah mile raaste
mere mude tu jahan

saans ki juban tale
sau baatein teri mile
aasra naseeb mein ho tera

do dil dono ki hai ye saazish
ya hai armaano ki ye guzarish

kaun ye sama gaya
