aj kahata huun phir miluunga tum se
tum mera intazar karati raho
aj ka aitabar karati raho

log kahate hain vaqt chalata hai
aur insan bhi badalata hai
kash ruk jaye vaqt aj ki rat
aur badale na koi aj ke bad

vaqt badale ye dil na badalega
tum se rishta kabhi na tuutega
tum hi khushbuu ho meri sanson ki
tum hi manzil ho mere sapanon ki

log bonte hain pyar ke sapane
aur sapane bikhar bhi jate hain
ek ehasas hi to hai ye vafa
aur ehasas mar bhi jate hain
