

 baat nikalegee to fir door talak jaayegee
log bewajah udaasee kaa sabab poochhenge
ye bhee poochhenge ke tum itanee pareshaan kyon ho
ungaliyaaan uthhegee sookhe hue baalon kee taraf

ek najar dekhenge gujare huye saalon kee taraf
choodiyon par bhee ke tnj kiye jaayenge
kaapaten haathon par bhee fikare kase jaayenge
log jaalim hain har ik baat kaa taanaa denge
baaton baaton men meraa jikr bhee le aayenge
un kee baaton kaa jaraa saa bhee asar mat lenaa
waranaa chehare ke taasur se samajh jaayenge
chaahe kuchh bhee ho sawaalaat naa karanaa unase
mere baare men koi baat naa karanaa unase
