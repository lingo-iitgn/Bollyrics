

 tu abhi waqif e gum naa ho ke abhi tera aehde shabab hai
meri chshme shok naa dekh tu ke yeh ansuwo ki kitab hai
naa meri nazar se nazar mike yeh itifaq azib hai
mujhe shok jameshabab ka teri ankh jameshrab hai
tere rukh par jo abhi nazar padi wo swae hushn se jal gai
tujhe dekh sakta nahi koi tera hushn khud hi nakqab hai
jahan dil khushi se jhuk gaya mera wahan sar bhi mene khushi se jhuka diya
mujhe aftab isse garaz nahi ki ye sabab hai ya azab hai
