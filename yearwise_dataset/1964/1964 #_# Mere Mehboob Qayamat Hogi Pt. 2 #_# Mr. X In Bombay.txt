

 mere mehboob qayamat hogi aaj rusavaa teri galiyon main mohabbat hogi
meri nazaren to gilaa karti hai tere dil ko bhi sanam tujhse shikaayat hogi

teri gali main aata sanam nagma vafaa kaa gaata sanam
tujhse suna naa jaataa sanam phir aaj idhar aayaa hoon magar
ye kehane main divaana khatm bas aaj ye vahshat hogi

aaj rusavaameri tarah tu aahen bhare tu bhi kisi se pyaar kare
or rahe wo tujhse pare tuune o sanam dhaayein hai sitam
to ye tu bhul na janaa ke naa tujhpe bhi inayat hogi
aaj rusavaa
