tumhaara kuch na bigadega magar dil tut jaayenge

ye bhigi raat ye bhigaa badan ye husn kaa alam
ye sab andaaz mil kar do jahaan ko lut jaayenge

ye najuk lab hai yaa aapas main do lipati hui kaliyaan
zaraa inako alag kar do tarannum foot jaayenge

hamaari jaan le legaa ye nichi aankh kaa jaadu
chalo acha hua mar kar jahaan se chhot jaayenge
