

 zindagi mujhako dikha de raasta 
tujhako meri hasaraton ka vaasta 
zindagi mujhako dikha 
bojh gam ka chajaata nahi
bebasi ka dukh saha jaata nahi
saara aalam ajanabi hai kya karoon 
ulajhanon mein kuch nazar aata nahi
o zindagi mujhako dikha 
mayakada jaaoon meri aadat nahi
haath phailaana meri fitarat nahi
ab chahoon to manzil paaoonga 
main nahi ya ab meri qismat nahi
o zindagi mujhako dikha
