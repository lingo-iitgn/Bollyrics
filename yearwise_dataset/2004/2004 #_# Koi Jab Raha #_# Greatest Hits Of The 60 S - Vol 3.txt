

 koi jab raah na paae mere sang aae
ke pag pag deep jalaae meri dosti mera pyaar

jivan ka yahi hai dastur
pyaar bina akemajabur
dosti ko maane to sab dukh dur
koi kaahe thokar khaae mere sang aae
dono ke hain roop hazaar
par meri sune jo sansaar
dosti hai bhaai to bahana hai pyaar
koi mat chain churaae mere sang aae
pyaar ka hai pyaar hi naam
kahin mira kahin ghanashyaam
dosti ka yaaro nahin koi daam
koi kahin dur na jaae mere sang aae
