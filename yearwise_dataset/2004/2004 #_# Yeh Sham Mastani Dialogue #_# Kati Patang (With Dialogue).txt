

 ye shaam mastaani madahosh kiye jaae
mujhe door koi khinche teri ore liye jaae

dur rahati he tu mere paas aati nahin
hothon pe tere kabhi pyaas aati nahin
aisa lage jaise ke tu hans ke jehar koi pie jaae

baat jab main karuun mujhe roak detii he kyon
teri mithi najar mujhe tok detii he kyon
teri haya teri shame teri kasam mere hont siye jaae

ek ruthi hui takadir jaise koi
khamosh aise he tu tasviir jaise koi
teri najar banake jubaan lekin tere paigaam die jaae
