

 kitni haseen zindagi he ye
hoton pe jaise kahani he
sadaa yahan kiskaa thikaana he
unki ravaani me janaa he
bahaaron ne harsu rang he bikheraa
ret kaa sehara ek pal kaa gheraa
ek din bikharana yahan

dil bhi kahin he pahaado me
todaa sa kahin he kinaaron me

phir ke chali he thandi hawaayen
kya tum miloge hunse jahaan leki jaae
bhigi bhigi palakhen kamasin adayen
maasoom cheharaa tera nahin bhul paae

tumhe bhi kabhi ye sataate hai
muskaraa ke dil ko churaate hai
jeene ko to dil ye chahta he
jane phir kyon sharmaata he
rukta nahin he sab kuch bataae
chupata nahin he dil jaan jaae
ab rahanaa kisko yahan
