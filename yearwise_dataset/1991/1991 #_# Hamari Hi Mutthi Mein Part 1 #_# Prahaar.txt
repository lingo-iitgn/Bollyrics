

 hamaari hi muththi main akaash sara
jab bhi khulegi chamakegaa tara
kabhi naa dhale jo  wo hi sitara
disha jis se pahachaane sansar sara

hatheli pe rekhaayen hai sab adhuri
kisne likhi hai nahin jaananaa hai
sulajhane unako na aayega koi
samajhnaa hai unako ye apana karam hai
apane karam se dikhaanaa hai sabko
khud kaa panapana  ubharna hai khudako
andheraa mitaae jo nanha sharaara  disha jisase

hamaare peeche koi aae naa aae
hamen hi to pehle pahuchanaa vahaan hai
jin par hai chalanaa nai peediyon ko
un hi raaston ko bananaa hamen hai
jo bhi saath aaye unhen saath le le
agar naa koi saath de to akele
sulagaa ke khud ko mitaa le andheraa  disha jisase
