

 chahat des se aanevaale ye to bataa ke sanam kaise hai
dilawaalon kii kya haalat hai yaar ke mausam kaise hai
kya ab bhi koi baton baton main rota hai
hans detaa hai us raah kii khushiyaan kisi hai
un galiyon ke gam kaise hai kya usne hamaara naam liya
kya usne hame kabhi yaad kiya kya
usne kabhi tujhse poocha kis haal main hai ham kaise hai
jugnu shabnam taaren banakar mere ansu dhund rahe ha
aanevaale tu hi bataa de mere humdam kaise hai
