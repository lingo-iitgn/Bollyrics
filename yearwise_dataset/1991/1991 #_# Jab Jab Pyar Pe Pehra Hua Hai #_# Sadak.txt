

 ho jab jab pyaar pe pahara huwa hai
pyaar aur bhee gahara  huwa hai
do pyaar karane vaalo ko ho ho ho
jab jab duniya tadapaayegee mohabbat badhatee jaayegee
ho kuchh bhee karale duniya yeh naa mit paayegee
mohabbat badhatee jaayegee

baadal ko barasane se bijalee ko chamakane se
koyee bhee rok naa paayega  
phulo ko mahakaane se bulbul ko chahakaane se
koyee bhee rok naa paayega  
jab jab ulfat kee raaho me duniya divaar uthaayegee
mohabbat badhatee jaayegee  

iss dil kee yaado ko mehbub ke vaado ko
koyee kya bandhe janjiro se  
chaahat ke khajaano ko najaro ke fasaano ko
koyee pa naa sake jaagiro se
jab jab duniya dilvaalo ko divaaro me chunavaayegee
mohabbat badhatee jaayegee
