

 din guzar gayaa aitabaar mein
raat kat gayii intazaar mein

woh mazaa kahaan vasleyaar mein
lutf jo milaa intazaar mein

unki ek nazar kaam kar gayee
hosh ab kahaan hoshiyaar mein

mere kabz mein aaina to hai
main hun aap ke ikhtiyaar mein

aankh to uthii phul ki taraf
dil ulajh gayaa husnkhaar mein

tumse kya kahein kitne gum sahe
ham ne bewafa tere pyaar mein

fikreaashiyaan har khizaam ki
aashiyaan jalaa har bahaar mein

kis tarah ye gum bhul jaaye hum
woh judaa hua is bahaar mein
