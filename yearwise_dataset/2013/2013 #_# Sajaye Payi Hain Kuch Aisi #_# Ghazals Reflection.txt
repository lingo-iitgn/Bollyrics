

 sazaye paayee hain kuchh aisi dil lagaane ki
na ab hain rone ki taakat na muskuraane ki

kadam kadam teri hasrat teri judai ka gham
tere bagair kisi tarah jee na paaye hum
hazaar koshishe ki tujh ko bhool jaane ki

wo khwaab khwaab si raahen wo aasuon ka safar
loota hain kis tarah lamhon me tere pyaar ka ghar
kahani kaise kahu dil ke toot jaane ki

na raas aaya kabhi hum ko pyaar ka mausam
tamaam umr raha intezar ka mausam
bahot hi aas thi dil ko bahaar aane kee
