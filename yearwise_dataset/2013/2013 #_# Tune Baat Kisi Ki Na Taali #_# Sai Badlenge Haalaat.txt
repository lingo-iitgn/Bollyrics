

 mujhse ab meri muhhobat ke phasaane na kaho
kitni bekaar umeedo ka sahara lekar
mene armaan sajaye the kisi ki khatir
kitni beraat tamanayo ki mubham khakar
apne khabo me basaye the kisi ki khatir

mujhse ab meri mohabbat ke phasane na kaho
mujhko kehne do ki mene unhe chaha hi nhi
kehne do aur woh mast nigahen jo mujhe bhool hi gayeen
main ne un mast nigahon ko saraha hi nahin
poetry
mujhko kahne do ki main aaj bhi aaj bhi ji saktee hoon
ishq nakam sahi zindagi nakam nahin
unko apnane ki khwahish unhen paane ki talab
