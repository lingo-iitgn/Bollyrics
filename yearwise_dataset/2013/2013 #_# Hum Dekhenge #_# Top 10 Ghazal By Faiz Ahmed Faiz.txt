

 laazim hai ki hum bhi dekhenge
woh din jiskaa ke waada hai
jo laueazl mein likha hai

jab zulmositam ke kohegaraan
rooi ki tarah udd jaayenge

hum mehkoomon ke paaon tale
jab dharti dhad dhad dhadkegi

aur ahlehukam ke sar oopar
jab bijli kad kad kadkegi

jab arzekhudaa ke kaabe se
sab but uthwaaey jaayenge

hum ahlesafaa mardoodeharam
masnad pe bithaaey 
sab taaj uchaaley sab takht giraaey 
bas naam rahega allah kaa
jo ghaayab bhi hai haazir bhi
jo manzar bhi hai naazir 
utthegaa analhaq kaa naara
jo main bhi hoon aur tum bhi ho

aur raaj karegi khalqekhuda
jo mai bhi hoon aur tum bhi ho
