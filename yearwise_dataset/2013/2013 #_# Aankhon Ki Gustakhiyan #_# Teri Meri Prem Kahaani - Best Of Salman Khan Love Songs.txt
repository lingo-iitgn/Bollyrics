ho aankhon kii gustakhiyaan maaf ho

ek tuk tumhe dekhati hai
jo baat kahana chaahe jubaan
tumse ye wo kahati hai

aankhon kii sharmohaya maaf ho

tumhe dekhake jhukaati hai

uthi aankhe jo baat na kah saki
jhuki aankhe wo kahati hai

aankhon kii ankhon kii gustakhiyaan maaf ho

kaajal kaa ek til tumhaare labon pe laga lulaa laa
haan chanda or suraj kii nazaron se tumko bachaa lu

o palkon kii chilman main aao main tumko chupa lo
khayalo kii yah shokhia maaf ho

har dam tumhe sochati hai
jab hosh main hota he jahaan
madahosh yah karaati hai

aankhon kii sharmohaya maaf ho

yah zindagi aapaki hi amaanat rahegi aay
dil main sadaa aapaki hi mohabbat rahegi aay
in saanso ko aapaki hi zarurat rahegi

is dil kii naadaniyaan maaf ho
yah mere kahan sunaati hai
yah pal pal jo hote hai bepal sanam
toh sapane nae bunati hai

ankhon kii gustakhiyaan maaf ho
sharmohaya maaf ho
