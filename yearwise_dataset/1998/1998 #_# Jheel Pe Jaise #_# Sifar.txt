

 jhil pe jaise naav chale aahista aahista
badal main aise ye chaand chhipe
kehane ko he ye bhi cheharaa

chhod aae peeche ham kisko kahan

unako khabar he kii ham he yahan

aae bahaaron main mahakaai raah
phir milane aayenge kya
bas thodi duuri ye rasta kahe
rukne lage phir chal die

jab wo hame yun dilaate he vaasta vaasta
rishte ham aise nibhaate he
raat se jaise koi subah

ab he nazar main ek aisa samaa

lekar hi aayenge tum ko yahan

anjaan rahon ke anjaan raahi

jaae to jaae kahan
tum ho salaamat ye dil yun kahe
chaahe mili yaa naa mili

dur se jo bulaae to aajaa naa aajaa naa
chaahe sawari bhi naa mili
ho sake to chalake hi aana
