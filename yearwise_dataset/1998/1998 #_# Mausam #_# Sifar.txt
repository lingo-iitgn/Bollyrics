

 mausam bhi yaar he gulshan gharbaar he
barkhaa kaa saath he kisi saugat he
ab jaae kab aae kaise bolo
jinke saath dil lagata he  unke saath holo

janaa kahan tha  hamako kahan ham chal die
choti choti hasaraton main ham ghul mil gayi
soch ke kya nikle the yah kya ham kar gayi
jo kehete wo nai karte naa in main reh gayi

rab yah jane ab kya hoga rasta he mushkil
kis chauraahey pe khadey he yaar

kitne armaan he  kaise anjaam he
barason kaa kaam he  lamhon pe naam he
der se aae jaise aae bolo
jinke saath dil lagata he  unke saath ho lo

mitti kaa bartan ho  bartan main ho sona
sone ke bartan main mitti aisa nai hona
aasmaan agar chat he  ye dharti bichhona
ambara ko de saaya koun  dharti kaa kya konaa
rab ye jane kaisaa hota kehna he mushkil
he us paar bhi gharbaar yaar

cheharaa hijaab he  gehara jawaab he
sach he yaa khwab he  sab kaa hisaab he
ab kaise sabko batalaaye bolo
jinke saath dil lagata he unke saath holo
