

 yuunhii tum mujhase baat karati ho
yaa koii pyaar kaa iraadaa hai
adaaen dil kii jaanataa hii nahiin
meraa hamadam bhii kitanaa saadaa hai
roz aatii ho tum kayaalon men 
zindagii men bhii merii aa jaao
biit jaae na ye savaalon men
is javaanii pe kuchh taras khaao
haaledil samajho sanam 
munh se na kahenge ham
hamaarii bhii koii maryaadaa hai yuunhii tum

ban gaii ho merii sadaa ke liye
yaa mujhe yuun hii tum banaatii ho
kahiin baahon men na bhar luun tumako
kyon mere hausale badhaatii ho
hausale aur karo faasale duur karo
paas aate na daro
dil na todenge apanaa vaadaa hai yuunhii tum

bholepan men hai vafaa kii khushabuu
isape sab kuchh na kyuun lutaauun main
meraa betaab dil ye kahataa hai
tere saae se lipat jaauun main
mujhase ye mel teraa 
na ho ik khel teraa
