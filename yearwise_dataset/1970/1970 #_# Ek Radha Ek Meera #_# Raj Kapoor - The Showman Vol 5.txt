

 ek radha ek mira donon ne shyaam ko chaha
antar kya donon kii chaah main bolo
ek prem divaani ek daras divaani

radha ne madhuban main dhundha mira ne man main paaya
radha jise kho baithi wo govind mira haath bik aayaa
ek murali ek paayal ek pagali ek ghayal
antar kya donon kii preet main bolo
ek surat lubhaani ek murat lubhaani
ek prem divaani ek daras divaani

mira ke prabhu giridhar nagar radha ke manmohan
radha nit shringaar kare or mira ban gayi jogan
ek rani ek daasi donon hari prem kii pyaasi
antar kya donon kii tripti main bolo
ek jeet na maani ek haar ne maani
ek prem divaani ek daras divaani
