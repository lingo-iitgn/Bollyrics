

 gore rang pe na itana gumaan kar 
gora rang do din mein dhal jaayega
main shama hoon tu hai parvaana 
mujhase pahale tu jal jaayega
gore rang pe

roop mit jaata hai ye pyaar ai diladaar nahi mitata
ho ho phool murajhaane se gulazaar o sarakaar nahi mitata
kya baat kahi hai hoy tauba 
ye dil beimaan machal jaayega
gore rang pe

o o aapako hai aisa inkaar to ye pyaar yaheen chhodo
o o pyaar ka mausam hai bekaar ki takaraar yaheen chhodo
haathon mein haath zara dedo 
baaton mein vaqt nikal jaayega
gore rang pe

o o main tujhe kar daaloon masaroor nashe men choor to maanoge
o o tumase main ho jaaoon kuch door e magarur ho manoge
tu laakh bacha mujhase daaman 
ye husn ka jaadu chal jaayega
gore rang pe
