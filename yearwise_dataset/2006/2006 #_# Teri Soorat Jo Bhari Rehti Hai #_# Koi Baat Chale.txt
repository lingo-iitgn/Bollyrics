

 teri surat jo bhari rehati hai
aankhon mein sada ajanabi log bhi pehchaane se lagate hai mujhe
tere rishton mein toh duniya hi duniya hi piro li maine
ek se ghar hai sabhi ek se hai baashinde
ajanabi shehar mein kuchh ajanabi lagata hi nahi
ek se dard hai sab ek se hi rishte hai
umrr ke khel mein ik tarfa hai yeh rassakashi ik sira mujhako diya hota toh kuchh baat bhi thi
mujhase tagada bhi aur hai saamane aata bhi nahi
saamane aaye mere dekha mujhe baat bhi ki
muskuraaye bhi puraane kisi rishte ke liye
kal ka akhbaar tha bas dekh liya rakh bhi diya
woh mere saath hi tha dur tak magr ik din mudake jo dekha
toh woh aur mere saath na tha
jeb phat jaaye toh kuchh sikke bhi kho jaate hai
chaudhave chaand ko phir aag lagi hai dekho
phir bahot der talak aaj ujaahoga
raakh ho jaayega jab phir se amaawas hogi
