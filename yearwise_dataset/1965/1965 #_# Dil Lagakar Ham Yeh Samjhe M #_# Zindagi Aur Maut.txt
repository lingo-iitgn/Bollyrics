

 dil laga kar hum ye samjey zindagi kya cheez hai
ishq kahetey hai kisey aur ashiqui kya cheez hai

haye ye ruksar ke sholey yeh bahen marmari  
aapse milke yeh do baaten samaj main aah gai
dhoop kiska naam hai aur chandni kya cheez hai

aap ki shouki ne kya kya roop diklaye hamey 
aapki aankhon ne kya kya jaam pilvaye hamey
hosh kho bhaidhe to jana bekhudi kya cheez hai
aap ki rahon mein jab se humne rakhha hai kadam 
hum ko ye mehsoos hota hai ki hain manzil pe hum
koi kya jaane mohabbat ki khushi kya cheez hai
dil lagakar hum yeh samjey zindagi kya cheez hai
