

 apana gham leke kahin aur na jaya jaye
ghar men bikhari hui chizon ko sajaya jaye

jin chiragon ko havaon ka koi khauf nahin
un charagon ko havaon se bachaya jaye

bag men jane ke adab hua karate hain
kisi titli ko na phulon se udaya jaye

ghar se masjid hai bohut dur chalo yun kar len
kisi rote hua bachche ko hansaya jaye
