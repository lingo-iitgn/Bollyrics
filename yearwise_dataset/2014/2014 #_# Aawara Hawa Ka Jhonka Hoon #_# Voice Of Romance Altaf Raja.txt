

 aawara hawa ka jhonka hoon aa nikhoon pal
do pal ke liye tum aaj to patthar barsaa lo kal
rooge mujh paagal ke liye khushbu na sahi rangat na sahi phir bhi hai
wafaa ka nazraanaa patjhad se churaa kar laaya
hoon do phool tere aanchal ke liye maikhwaar ko itna hosh kahaan rishte
ki hakeekat ko samjhe beti ka saudaa kar daala
daru ki ik botal ke liye dil aur jigar to
kuchh bhi nahi ek baar ishaara to kar de mai
khud ko jabhi saktaa hoon teri aankhon ke
kaajal ke liye hum log hai aise deewaane jo
zid pe kabhi aa jaaye to sehra ke utha kar
laayenge jhankaar teri paayal ke liye ye khel tamaasha lagta hai taqdeer ke gulshan ka
shaayad kaaten hai mere daaman ke liye aur
phool tere aanchal ke liye
