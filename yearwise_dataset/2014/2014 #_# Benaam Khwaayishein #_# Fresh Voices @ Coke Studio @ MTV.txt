

 benaam si khwaishein
aawaz na mile
bandishein kyun khwaab pe
parwaaz na jaane hai par maane dil na tu na mere bebasi yeh pukaar rahi hai aa saajan benaam si khwaishein aawaz na mile

chaand teri roshni aftaab se hai magar
chaah ke bhi na mile hai dono ki nazar
duriyaan din raat ki hein tai na ho fasle
benaam si khwaishein aawaz na mile

patjhad jaaye barkha aaye ho bahar
mausam badalte rahe
dil ke nagar jo basi sard hawaein
kyun na jaayeinaa ja aa bhi ja mausam katte na birah kebenaam si khwaishein aawaz na mile
