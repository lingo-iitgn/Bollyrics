

 tera meraa saath ho
or pyaar bhari raat ho
kabhi bhuule naa dono ko bhi
ek aisi mulaakaat ho
main dil tu meri dhadkan
bas tu hi meraa jivan
jeena nahi jeena tere bin haae

       
      
     
       
      
     

ek pal dekhun naa main jo tujh ko
kahin chaiyn nahin paaun
pyaar tera dil meraa maange sadaa
dil ke jahaan pe raaj he tera
tuje kaise batalaauun
haan jaanata he ye meraa khuda
main dekhun teri raahen
meri tere sang vafaayen
jeena nahi jeena tere bin haae

      
       
     
      
       
     

akhiyon me tera pyaar he bastaa
or dil main bhi he tu
kaise hoon pal bhi main tujh se juda
chur naa kar khwab suhane
jo dekhe pyaar main
naam tere he likh di vafaa
tu apana bana le mujhako
bahon me chupa le mujhako
jeena nahi jeena tere bin haae
