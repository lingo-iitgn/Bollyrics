

 bulley nu samjhawanr ayaan bhenra te bharjayiya
man le bulleya saada kehnra chhadtay palraiya
aale nabi o laat nabi nu tu kyun lika layiya
jera sanu syed sayyad dozakh mile sajaiya
bandya ho bandya bandya ho  

rayi sayi sabhi thayi rabdiya beparwaiya

sohniya pare hatayiya te tujhay de kar layiya
je tu loray pak bahara chak kar ho ja layiya
bullay shah di zaat ki buchhri shak kar ho rajjayiya
bandya ho bandya bandya ho  

i face the world with a smile no one knows what is hidden inside
they see only happiness
they cant see the tears i ve cried
bandya ho bandya bandya ho  

viren swami
