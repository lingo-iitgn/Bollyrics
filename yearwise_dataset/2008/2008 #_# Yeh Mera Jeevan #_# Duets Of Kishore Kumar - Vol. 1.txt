

 ye mera jeevan tere liye hai
jeevan ka sapana tere liye hai
maang le hansake kya chaahiye tujhe
meri to duniya tere liye hai

daali pe baithi chhoti si chidiya
kaandhe pe mere nanhi si gudiya
chidiya se bhi sundar meri gudiyaa ye mera 
dekha tha sapana jo zindagi ka
ye bhi to vaisa hi din hai khushi ka
jeene ka dhang tujhase mainne seekhaa ye mera 
maano badal jaaye donon ki manzil
phir bhi rahega sath mera dil
chaahenge to milana nahi mushkil ye mera
