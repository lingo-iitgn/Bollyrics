

 mubarak ho sabko samaa ye suhana
main khush hoon mere aasuonpe naa janaa
main to divaana divaana divaana

hajaaron tarah ke ye hote hai ansu
agar dil main gam ho to rote hai ansu
khushi main bhi aankhe bheegote hai ansu
inhe jaan saktaa nahin ye zamana

ye shehnaiyaan de rahi he duhai
koi cheez apni hui he paraai
kisi se milan he kisi se judaai
nae rishton ne toda naata pooraana

ye bole samay kii nadi kaa bahaav
ye babul kii galiyaan ye maanjhi kii naav
chali ho to gori suno bhul jaao
na phir yaad karnaa na phir yaad aana
