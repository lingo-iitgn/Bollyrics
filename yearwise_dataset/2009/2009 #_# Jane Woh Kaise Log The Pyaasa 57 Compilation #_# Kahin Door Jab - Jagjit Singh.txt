

 jaane wo kaise log the jinake pyaar ko pyaar mila
hamane to jab kaliyaan maangi kaanton ka haar mila

khushiyon ki mnjil dhundhi to gam ki gard mili
chaahat ke nagmen chaahe to ahen sard mili
dil ke bojh ko duna kar gaya jo gamakhwaar mila

bichhad gaya har saathi de kar pal do pal ka saath
kisako furasat hai jo thaame diwaanon ka haath
hamako apana saaya tak aksar bezaar mila

is ko hi jina kahate hain to yun hi ji lenge
uf na karenge lab si lenge ansu pi lenge
gam se ab ghabaraana kaisa gam sau baar mi
